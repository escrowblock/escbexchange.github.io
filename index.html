F.A.Q

When will ESCB Exchange Platform be launched?

We expect to launch ESCB exchange platform in 1-2 months after airdrop will be ended. Airdrop has to take not more than 1-2 months. In common words, we will launch the exchange platform at least in test mode in 2-4 months.

Which platforms will you support?

We are working on web-client, android and ios client apps in the first priority./p>

For what reasons do you have splitting on advanced and simple API?

Advanced API can be used for building other crypto-fiat platforms, such as ESCB platform. Simple API can be used for a quick exchange process for embedded widgets.

Should I be registered on your exchange platform?

It is desired, but not necessary. Any counterparty will have rating related with many indicators, KYC process will be one from such indicator.

Which legal form will you use for processing fiat transactions?

We are the software company and for our activities, we don't need to use a special form of organization and get licences, because we provide only software. All crypto-fiat transactions will happen between counterparties directly.

How will you protect counterparties from scam activities?

We have developed "Trust system" based on multisign wallet hierarchy. It allows protecting each side of a deal from scam scheme. Also, we use machine learning for the risk management system.

Will you allow to conduct anonimity transactions?

We don't demand to do mandatory KYC process, but it is allowing to decrease fees on the platform. Than less information "Trust system" knows about counterparties, the more fee will be on such deal.
